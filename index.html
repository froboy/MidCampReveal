Look familiar?

Most of the questions people ask me are something like "how do I get started with Drupal?" There are plenty of resources out there, but at this point that seems to be the problem.

The Problem:

The number of resources is skyrocketing but there's no good way for an amature to wade through the mountains of information—some great and some mediocre.

How do we get from this...

We need to whittle down the choices to move away from this ‘paradox of choice’...

...to this?

...to find one clear direction forward. This session will not break any new ground. No great new insights will be shared. We'll simply take a quick ride through the best resources a generalist needs to get started on the right path with Drupal, in one hour or less, starting from "The Internet" and working our way down.
